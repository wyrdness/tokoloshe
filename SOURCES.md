## Cultural Sensitivity Note

When researching the tokoloshe, consult:
- Local sangomas (traditional healers) with proper respect and protocol
- Academic experts in Zulu and Xhosa cultures
- Cultural authorities in affected communities
- Ethical guidelines for researching living belief systems

Do not:
- Exploit beliefs for entertainment
- Mock or trivialize serious cultural practices
- Ignore the real-world implications of these beliefs
- Appropriate cultural elements without permission
